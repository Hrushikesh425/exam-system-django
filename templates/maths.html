Mathematics

Class 11 Maths is an introduction to advance math. Further, it is quite different from the maths of class 9 and 10. Chapter 1 is about sets, which teaches intersection and union, types, and operation of sets. Chapter 2 explains relations and functions such as evaluating combining functions, etc. Chapter 3 describes trigonometric functions that relate to sin, cos, tan, etc. Chapter 4 defines the principles of mathematical induction. Chapter 5 describes complex numbers and quadratic equations. Chapter 6 teaches about linear inequalities such as one-step, two-step, multi-step inequalities. Chapter 7 explains permutations and combination and fundamental principles of counting. Chapter 8 teaches the binomial theorem for positive integral indices. Chapter 9 defines the sequence and series. Also, it teaches basic sigma notation, finite geometric series, and many other concepts. Chapter 10 is about straight lines that teach many topics such as horizontal and vertical lines, equations of parallel and perpendicular lines, x-intercepts and y-intercepts, etc. Chapter 11 describes conic sections, the equation of an ellipse, features of a circle, foci of an ellipse, standard and expanded equation of a circle, and much more. Chapter 12 gives an introduction to three-dimensional geometry, its role, and measures. Chapter 13 talks about limits and derivatives their features and many other related concepts. Chapter 14 teaches mathematical reasoning. Chapter 15 is about statistics but not general statistics. Lastly, chapter 16 is about probability, which is the possibility of something.

Class 12 Maths aims to solidify students in the field of Maths. There are 13 chapters in Maths of class 12. The first chapter is Relations and Functions which involves the concept of relation and functions, domain, co-domain, range, etc. Inverse Trigonometric Functions chapter deals with restrictions on domains and trigonometric functions ranges that facilitate visibility of their inverses. Furthermore, the Matrix chapter takes you deeper into the concept of matrix as well as matrix algebra. In the Determinants chapter, you will learn about determinants which are up to order three only with real entries. Continuity and Differentiability is the fifth chapter which deals with important concepts of continuity, differentiability, and the relations existing between them. In the Application of Derivatives chapter, you will go through the derivative of composite functions, exponential functions, implicit functions, logarithmic functions, and inverse trigonometric functions. Moreover, the Integrals chapter revolves around the concept of Integral. Application of Integrals is another chapter here and it will teach you about the specific application of integrals. Differential Equations chapter will teach you the basic concepts of differential equations. Vector Algebra revolves around the concept of vectors. Three Dimensional geometry is also included in maths of class 12 which deals with the study of direction cosines and direction ratios related to a line. This chapter also has equations of lines and planes. Linear Programming teaches linear programming by graphical method only. Finally, Probability chapter deals with the concepts of probability and its related aspects.
